The Studio

Albuquerque Pinho and Co is a collaborative architecture and design studio founded in 2020 by Luís Albuquerque Pinho. Based in Porto, the studio works across the fields of architecture, object design, wellbeing, critical thinking, and aesthetics, performing nationally and internationally. Its multidisciplinary practice embraces design as a cultural and ethical act — shaping not only the physical space, but also the relational spheres and ecological landscapes.

Approach

Beyond architectural practice, APco embraces an expanded practice, collaborating across disciplines and media, reflecting the studio’s commitment to cultural engagement and experimentation, pushing architecture’s boundaries into art, public space, and collective memory.  Besides architectural work, the studio offers consulting in construction processes, providing technical guidance in international contexts.

With a primary focus on housing, public cultural buildings and urban planning, the studio’s work is guided by a strong ethos of generosity and economy. Each project aspires to serve life and community, foster use and appropriation, challenging the conventional norms through precise yet open-ended design strategies, placing the principles of sustainability at the core if its designs and philosophy. Through the selection of materials, construction methods, and energy use, the studio’s work reflects a commitment to architecture’s role in shaping climate-conscious and regenerative futures.  APco has a growing interest in preserving cultural memory through contemporary design strategies. The preservation of cultural heritage and respect for traditional construction methods and craft, reinterpreting vernacular techniques and using local materials and labor, building for the future in dialogue with the past. Albuquerque Pinho and Co continues to evolve as a critical and creative platform, committed to developing architectural practices that are not only intellectually and socially rigorous, but also deeply responsive to the environmental challenges of our time.
